## Decisions

### Visual Style Direction
Adopt the "X: AI Tech Start-Up" aesthetic — clean, minimal, professional with blue-dominant palette.

**Color System:**
- Primary blue: #0057FF with dark states #003ECC, #002F9A
- Neutral foundation: #F9F9F9 (light bg) to #191919 (dark bg)
- Supporting: Orange #F97C00, Green #028901, Red #D00D00
- Cards: white backgrounds with subtle 1px borders, soft shadows (`0 1px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`)

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif with clear weight hierarchy (light 300 to bold 700+)
- Large hero text (up to 64px), scaled heading hierarchy
- 8px base spacing unit

**Components:**
- Buttons: primary (blue bg), secondary (white + border), tertiary (light blue)
- Cards: clean white bg, subtle 1px borders, soft shadows, 12px border radius
- Inputs: consistent height, 4px border radius, blue focus states
- Modals: 12px border radius, gentle and approachable

**Layout:**
- Grid-based responsive with standardized 8px spacing units
- Professional, enterprise feel without sacrificing approachability
- Dark mode: #191919 base with inverted neutral scale
