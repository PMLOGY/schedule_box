**Create skip-link.tsx:**
A simple component that renders an `<a href="#main-content">` link. Styling: `sr-only` by default, on focus: `focus:not-sr-only focus:fixed focus:top-4 focus:left-4 focus:z-[100] focus:px-4 focus:py-2 focus:bg-primary focus:text-primary-foreground focus:rounded-md focus:shadow-lg focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-ring`. Text: use `useTranslations('accessibility')` and render `t('skipToContent')`. This provides keyboard-only users a way to bypass navigation.

- Wrap the header in a consistent structure: locale switcher sits to the left of UserMenu in the right-side div.
