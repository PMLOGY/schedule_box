<objective>
Create auth email library and wire password reset and email verification sends into their respective auth routes.

Purpose: Password reset and email verification are currently no-ops — the routes generate tokens and store them in Redis but never send the actual email. This makes both flows non-functional in production. Auth emails use direct nodemailer (not RabbitMQ) because they are synchronous security flows where latency matters.

Output: `apps/web/lib/email/auth-emails.ts` with `sendPasswordResetEmail` and `sendEmailVerificationEmail` functions; both auth routes updated to call these functions.
</objective>
